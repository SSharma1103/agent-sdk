# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a respectful, harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be kind and constructive.
- Assume good intent, but accept feedback when impact differs from intent.
- Keep discussions focused on the project and its users.
- Respect differing experience levels and backgrounds.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Personal attacks or sustained disruption.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Any conduct that would reasonably make contributors feel unsafe or unwelcome.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject contributions, or ban participants who violate this code of conduct.

To report a concern, use the contact method listed in `SECURITY.md` or open a private maintainer channel if available.
